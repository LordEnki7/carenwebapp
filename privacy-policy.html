C.A.R.E.N.™ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

1. Introduction

C.A.R.E.N.™ (Citizen Assistance for Roadside Emergencies and Navigation) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Information

Why we collect it: We collect precise location data to provide state-specific legal rights information and emergency assistance services.

How we use it:

  • Determine your current state for relevant legal rights display
  • Share location coordinates with emergency contacts when you activate emergency features
  • Provide location-aware legal guidance and resources
  • Coordinate roadside assistance services

Data retention: Location data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored unless you save it as part of an incident report.

2.2 Camera and Microphone Access

Why we collect it: We access your camera and microphone for evidence documentation and voice command features.

How we use it:

  • Record audio and video evidence during incidents
  • Process voice commands for hands-free operation
  • Document incidents with photo and video evidence

Data retention: Recordings are stored locally on your device and in your secure account. You control what gets shared and can delete recordings at any time.

2.3 Personal Information

We collect information you provide when creating an account:

  • Name and email address
  • Emergency contact information
  • Subscription and payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • User preferences and settings

2.4 Bluetooth and Device Connectivity

Why we collect it: We access Bluetooth to connect with compatible devices for hands-free operation.

How we use it:

  • Connect to hands-free car systems and audio devices
  • Enable voice command processing through connected devices
  • Coordinate multi-device emergency response

Data retention: Bluetooth connection data is temporary and not stored permanently.

2.5 Biometric Information

Why we collect it: Optional biometric authentication (facial recognition) for secure app access.

How we use it:

  • Secure app access during emergencies
  • Quick authentication for time-sensitive situations

Data retention: Biometric templates are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

2.6 Communication Data

We process communications for emergency and legal services:

  • SMS and email notifications to emergency contacts
  • Secure messaging with attorney network partners
  • Voice command transcriptions for app functionality
  • Real-time synchronization data across your devices

2.7 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the app:

  • Feature usage analytics
  • App performance and crash reports (via Firebase)
  • User interaction patterns for app improvement
  • Voice command accuracy and usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Emergency Services: Provide real-time assistance and coordinate with emergency contacts
  • Legal Protection: Display relevant legal rights based on your location
  • Account Management: Manage your subscription and account preferences
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Support: Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Safety: Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized use

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

4.1 Emergency Situations

When you activate emergency features, we may share your location and incident details with:

  • Your designated emergency contacts
  • Attorney network partners (if you request legal assistance)
  • Roadside assistance providers (if you request such services)

4.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services:

  • Replit: Application hosting and authentication
  • Firebase: Crash reporting and analytics
  • Stripe: Secure payment processing
  • SendGrid: Email delivery services
  • TextBelt/Twilio: SMS emergency notifications
  • OpenStreetMap: Location geocoding services
  • Attorney Network Partners: Legal consultation services (when requested)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-End Encryption: AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive communications and data
  • Transport Security: All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest with industry-standard protocols
  • Local Storage: Biometric data and sensitive recordings stored locally on your device
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Real-time Sync Security: WebSocket connections use secure protocols and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates
  • GDPR Compliance: Full compliance with European data protection standards

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Location Data: Real-time processing only, not permanently stored unless saved in incident reports
  • Recordings: Stored locally and in your secure account until you delete them
  • Biometric Data: Stored only on your local device, never transmitted or stored on servers
  • Communication Data: Encrypted attorney communications retained per legal requirements
  • Voice Commands: Processed in real-time, temporary transcriptions deleted after processing
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained for app improvement
  • Emergency Data: Incident reports retained until you choose to delete them

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or other features

8. Children's Privacy

C.A.R.E.N.™ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. International Users

C.A.R.E.N.™ is primarily designed for users in the United States. If you use our service from other countries, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
