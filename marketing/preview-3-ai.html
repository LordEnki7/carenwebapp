Can police search my car without my permission?
🤖 AI ASSISTANT
No. In Ohio, officers cannot search your vehicle without probable cause, a warrant, or your voluntary consent. You have the right to say:

"I do not consent to searches."

This is protected under the 4th Amendment and Ohio Rev. Code § 2933.51.
YOU
Can I record the police?
🤖 AI ASSISTANT
Yes. Ohio is a one-party consent state. You can legally record police performing their duties in public spaces. This was affirmed in State v. Bock (2017).
