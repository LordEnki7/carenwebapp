⚖️ Your Rights in Ohio

📍 Cuyahoga County, OH
🚗 Traffic Stops
You must provide license, registration & insurance. You do NOT have to answer questions about where you're going. Ohio Rev. Code § 4549.02
📹 Recording Rights
Ohio is a one-party consent state. You may legally record police officers performing their duties in public. State v. Bock (2017)
🔍 Search & Seizure
Officers cannot search your vehicle without probable cause, a warrant, or your consent. You have the right to refuse a search. Ohio Rev. Code § 2933.51
🛡️ Police Accountability
Officers must identify themselves when asked. You have the right to file complaints with Internal Affairs. Cleveland Municipal Code § 623.01
📱 Phone Privacy
Police need a warrant to search your phone, per Riley v. California (2014). Do not unlock your device voluntarily.
