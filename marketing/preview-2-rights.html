📍 Cuyahoga County, OH
🚗 Traffic Stops
You must provide license, registration & insurance. You do NOT have to answer questions about where you're going. Ohio Rev. Code § 4549.02
📹 Recording Rights
Ohio is a one-party consent state. You may legally record police officers in public. State v. Bock (2017)
🔍 Search & Seizure
Officers cannot search without probable cause, warrant, or consent. You may refuse. Ohio Rev. Code § 2933.51
🛡️ Police Accountability
Officers must identify themselves when asked. You may file complaints with Internal Affairs.
📱 Phone Privacy
Police need a warrant to search your phone. Riley v. California (2014). Do not unlock voluntarily.
