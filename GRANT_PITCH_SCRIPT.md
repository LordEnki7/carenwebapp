# C.A.R.E.N.™ Alert — Grant Pitch Script
*For HeyGen avatar video. Estimated runtime: 60–90 seconds at natural speaking pace (~170 words spoken).*
*Swap [GRANT NAME] and [ORGANIZATION] for each application.*

---

## SCRIPT

---

Hi, I'm [YOUR NAME], founder of C.A.R.E.N. Alert —
the first AI-powered platform that protects families during traffic stops and roadside emergencies.

When a driver says "I'm being pulled over,"
C.A.R.E.N. activates instantly.
It detects their location, reads their state-specific legal rights through a Bluetooth earpiece,
begins recording from multiple cameras,
and notifies emergency contacts — all hands-free.

We cover all 50 states, 467 constitutional protections,
with an AI legal assistant, attorney video connect, and court-ready evidence packaging
built right in.

C.A.R.E.N. is live today at carenalert.com.
This is not a concept — it's a working product with real users, real infrastructure,
and a mobile app actively in development.

The [GRANT NAME] from [ORGANIZATION] would accelerate our iOS and Android launch,
expand our attorney network into underserved communities,
and put this protection in the hands of the families who need it most.

People deserve to know their rights in the moment they need them.
C.A.R.E.N. makes that possible.

Thank you.

---

*[END OF SCRIPT]*

---

## DELIVERY NOTES FOR HEYGEN

- **Pace**: Slow and steady. Do not rush. Let each section breathe.
- **Tone**: Confident, warm, serious — not salesy. You believe in this.
- **Pauses**: Add a natural pause after each `---` section break.
- **Emphasis**: The phrases *"right there, in real time, in their ear"* and *"This is not a concept. This is a working product."* are your strongest lines — slow down on those.
- **Eye contact**: Look directly at camera the whole time.
- **Length**: At a comfortable pace this runs approximately 2.5 minutes — ideal for grant video submissions.

## OPTIONAL ADD-ON LINES (use if the grant focuses on specific areas)

**If the grant is focused on technology / innovation:**
> "C.A.R.E.N. is one of the first platforms to combine AI, real-time GPS legal data,
> and cloud-based evidence preservation into a single consumer mobile experience.
> We are building infrastructure that did not exist before."

**If the grant is focused on social equity / underserved communities:**
> "Black and brown drivers are disproportionately impacted during traffic stops.
> C.A.R.E.N. was built with those families in mind.
> Every feature — from voice activation to Bluetooth earpiece coaching —
> was designed so that knowledge of your rights doesn't depend on
> whether you had the money to hire an attorney first."

**If the grant is focused on women or minority-owned business:**
> "As a [minority / woman / veteran]-owned business,
> we understand firsthand why tools like C.A.R.E.N. are necessary —
> and why they need to be built by people who live these experiences."

**If the grant is focused on job creation:**
> "This funding will allow us to hire developers, community outreach coordinators,
> and attorney network managers — creating real jobs rooted in public safety technology."
